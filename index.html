Best Feature of Pak Forces

Pak Army

Pakistan Army is its strong focus on counterterrorism operations. Over the years, the Pakistan Army has been heavily involved in combating terrorism and insurgency, particularly in regions like the tribal areas along the Afghan border. Through operations like Zarb-e-Azb and Radd-ul-Fasaad, the army has played a significant role in stabilizing conflict zones and improving internal security within Pakistan.

Pak Air Force

Pakistan Air Force (PAF) is its JF-17 Thunder multirole combat aircraft, which was developed in collaboration with China. The JF-17 is a lightweight, single-engine fighter that serves both air-to-air and air-to-ground roles. It's equipped with modern avionics, weapons systems, and has the capability to engage in a variety of combat scenarios. This aircraft is a cornerstone ofPakistan's defense strategy, providing the Air Force with a balance of affordability and advanced technology.

Pak Navy

Pakistan Navy is its strong focus on maritime security, especially through the development of its submarine force. The Pakistan Navy operates a fleet of submarines, including conventional diesel-electric submarines, which are critical for strategic deterrence, coastal defense, and intelligence gathering. This capability allows the Pakistan Navy to maintain a strong undersea presence, safeguarding the country's coastline and territorial waters.
